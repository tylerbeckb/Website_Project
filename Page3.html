Plant Trees


Help by either planting or donating money to a charity that will plant a tree for you. This will help as the tree you planted will absorb CO2 to produce oxygen. It is the most simple but effective way that you can do it home. You can make it a community activity, then the whole community can plant a tree which would make a real difference.

There are mulitple charities that you cann plant a tree with like the national trust. To donate search 'plant a tree' into google to press the button below to go straight to the national trust donation page.

Educate People


Educating people about the impacts of deforestation will help raise awareness of this major issue. This will increase the chance action will take place due too as an increasingly amount of people will be putting pressure on governments to act.

Education can create a multiplier effect where if you educate one person they might educate another which will get more people to act to help stop deforestation.
