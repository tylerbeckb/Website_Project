UK Deforestation Problem

A major problem that is burning the heart of the UK

What is deforestation?

Deforestation is the activity of cutting down trees and clearing forests purposefully. The main driver is to create an area for agriculture or to collect wood as a resource. For example:

  • Wood for fuel
  • Palm oil plantation
  • Animal grazing
