Stand For Change


Fundraising is such an important way to help support change and keep the charities fighting for what is right.

It can take on many forms and we will be discussing many ways you could fundraise for rainforest charities.


Click on the bullet points below for more information.

Run a Marathon

  • • Pick a Marathon

    • Go to find a race for all marathons running each year
  • • Train

    • - Create a training plan for 24 weeks
    - Work yourself up by running little and often
    - Pace yourself
    - Take rest days
  • • Set up Fundraising Page

    • - Use GoFundMe, set up a page using the steps on the website
    - Use Facebook, they have a good fundraiser page which can be easily shared with your following
    - Share the pages across all platforms and tell people about the page
  • • Run

    • - Have fun and give it your best shot!!
Bake Sale
This is a quick and easy way to fundraise. Anyone can join in all you have to do is bake some treats! A bake sale can be a done together as a community which gives joy to everyone around you. Share around that you're having one and have fun!
Bungee Jump
This is a daredevil activty that the extreme fundraises can give ago. It's a scary thing to do but with the motivation of fundraising you can complete the task and rasie some money with it. Create a sponsorship page then share it around. You will be ready.
Garage Sale
A great way to get rid of unwanted items and raise some money for charity. There will be unwanted toys or clothes that you can sell, it is very easy to set up just create some awareness and people will come to help you raise some money.
