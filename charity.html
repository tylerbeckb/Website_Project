Cool Earth


Cool Earth charity aims to protect as most of the forest as possible to try to reduce deforestation. They also give money to the indigenous people of the rainforest to stop illegal logging and to protect the forest. They have saved over 177 million tonnes of carbon to be released into the atmosphere.

They use many solutions to this problem for example: wildfire prevention; carbon capturing; forest monitoring. Forest monitoring is the act of finding trends and patterns in data and making them accessible to everyone. WWF


WWF aims to protect everything in the ecosystem and try to regenerate nature wherever it is damaged. Some of their projects are: protesting fossil fuels; making sustainable food; limiting the destruction of forests. Try to build a sustainble future for humanity by tackling climate change and global hunger. One of their main achievments is restoring 1.2 milllion seagrass in the UK which benefits the ocean health dramatically.

To help with their goal you can donate directly to them or adopt an endangered animal. Moreover, you can become a member and be able volunteer. This is a summary of (WWF.2023) and you can find out more by clicking the image.



Rainforest Trust


Rainforest Trust aims to protect forests across the globe. They have protected over 40 million acres and has kept the habitats for all animals that live there. Saved many endangered aniamls habitats from destruction, perserving our ecosystem. Furthmore, people are being protected too as they are a major part of protecting the forest by policing the area for illegal logging. Rainforest trust gets the ownership of the land to protect the wildlife and gives natural resources to the people that live there.
