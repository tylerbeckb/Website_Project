Amazon Road


Brazil are going to pave a road that goes straight through the Amazon rainforest. Researches say that this road will cause further deforestation to the Amazon, destroying the ecosystem. The paved road will help with connecting Brazils areas however, deforestation will increase dramaticly as its easier to get machines to the rainforest.

This area is one of the Amazons most untouched areas, with the introduction into better road quality the area will not be as untouched as trees will need to get cleared for the road to get paved.

Amazon Degragation


The guardian has reported that a third of the Amazon rainforest is being degraded by droughts and human causes. Droughts are causing water shortages across the rainforest making the trees more fammable and prone to forest fires. Furthermore, it stops new plants from growing which would replenish biodiversity

Human causes like logging is destroying the Amazon increasingly reducing the capacity to store carbon. This needs to slow down as the guardian states that 17% of the rainforest has been lost in the past century.

This information has been a summary from (guardian, 2023)
