Sumatra Rainforest

Sumatra rainforest is one of the most rapidly deforested places on the planet due to illegal logging and not enough funding to help stop it. Indonesia contribute over a 3rd of global carbon emissions from deforestation. Palm oil plantations are being fuelled by demand which contributes heavily to Indonesian deforestation. 50 million people live in the forest and rely on it to survive. However, with the shift to destroying the forest many are straving or forced to leave their home as nature gets burnt around them.
